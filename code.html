Clast of Clans townhall Bases

Townhall Level 7

At Seventh level the Town Hall has 3,200Hit Points, the upgrade cost is 1,200,000Gold and last for 6 days. After finishing of the upgrade You will get the Experience: 720Experience. At this Level the Town Hall can store the following maximum amount of Resources: 500,000Gold, 500,000Elixir and 2,500Dark Elixir. Seventh level of the Town Hall allows you to build 17 additional Buildings (Total is 70, including the Town Hall itself, Traps and 5 Builders Huts)

Townhall level 11

At Eleventh level the Town Hall has 6,800Hit Points, the upgrade cost is 7,000,000Gold and last for 14 days. After finishing of the upgrade You will get the Experience: 1,099Experience. At this Level the Town Hall can store the following maximum amount of Resources: 2,000,000Gold, 2,000,000Elixir and 20,000Dark Elixir. Eleventh level of the Town Hall allows you to build 6 additional Buildings (Total is 119, including the Town Hall itself, Traps and 5 Builders Huts).
